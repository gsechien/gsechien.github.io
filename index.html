Welcome!

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Management at National Sun Yat-sen University. I completed my Ph.D. in the School of Computing and Information at the University of Pittsburgh, and was an adjunct faculty in this founding iSchool. My background spans the field of Human-Automation Collaboration, including HCI, UX, Human-Robot Systems, Technology Acceptance across Cultures.

My current research projects examine the Impact of Cultural Dynamics in Trusting Human-Humanoid Robot Interaction. I plan to conduct empirical studies and measure the cultural effects in Taiwan, U.S., Germany, Turkey, and South Korea. Welcome any opportunities to explore the possibilities of collaborating with you!
